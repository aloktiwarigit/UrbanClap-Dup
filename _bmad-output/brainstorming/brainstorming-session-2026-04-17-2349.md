# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** Mary (Strategic Business Analyst)
**Participant:** Alokt
**Date:** 2026-04-17 23:49 IST
**Project:** homeservices-mvp (UC-parity home services marketplace, small-scale pilot)

## Session Overview

**Topic:** Small-scale (1 city max) 3-app home services platform — Customer + Technician + Owner — replicating Urban Company's core business flow on ₹0 infrastructure, with impeccable world-class UI/UX as the competitive wedge vs UC.

**Goals:**
1. Prioritized differentiator ideas (especially UX angles that beat UC's 1.4★ experience)
2. Open questions to feed Phase 2 PRD
3. Unconventional angles (within scope — no out-of-app, no edge-case personas)
4. Stress-test assumptions from the product brief

### Session Setup

The participant confirmed a **scope narrowing** from the product brief's full UC parity (multi-city, subscriptions, native products, instant service, ML dispatch) to a **core-loop MVP** focused on the minimal viable three-app model:

- **Customer app:** service catalogue, book, live-track, pay, review, history — zero visibility into owner operations
- **Technician app:** receive job offers, order details, earnings from owner, ratings, history — zero visibility into owner finance
- **Owner web:** full-visibility control plane — every service, order, tech, payment, complaint

**Key quote from Alokt:**
> "think about it clearly from domain user prospective what all features they would be looking for like customer would not need any detail for owner but need to know everything about the service and history ad payment and when technician is reaching .. reviews technician reviews picking the right one etc .. technician will be looking for order details , payment with owner and other details .. similarly owner will have full view .. like service/order/payment/technician etc"

This role-by-role perspective drives the entire brainstorming design.

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** Progressive Technique Flow (divergent → analytical → convergent → stress-test)
**Journey Design:** Systematic development from expansive per-role ideation through prioritised differentiators

**Progressive Techniques:**

| Phase | Technique | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Expansive Exploration | **Role Playing** | Embody each role separately; generate 100+ ideas split ~35/35/30 across Customer/Technician/Owner |
| 2 — Pattern Recognition | **Mind Mapping** | Cluster ideas into feature themes per role + identify cross-role flows; surface gaps |
| 3 — Idea Development | **SCAMPER Method** | Put top themes through Substitute/Combine/Adapt/Modify/Put-to-other-uses/Eliminate/Reverse lenses — with every pass asking "how does this beat UC's 1.4★ experience?" |
| 4 — Action Planning | **Reverse Brainstorming** (Pre-mortem) | Flip: "Project failed at month 12 — what killed it?" Stress-test top features + brief assumptions; convert into prioritised differentiators + open questions for PRD |

**Journey Rationale:** The progressive flow mirrors natural creative work — flood the page first (Role Playing), organise (Mind Mapping), refine (SCAMPER), stress-test (Reverse Brainstorming). It produces the exact three deliverables requested: prioritised differentiators, open questions for PRD, stress-tested assumptions.
